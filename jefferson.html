Welcome to Hamiltasteroids

  • This is Hamilton.

    He is locked in a war of words with Jefferson.

  • This is Jefferson.

    He is locked in a war of words with Hamilton.

  • The game is Asteroids.

    You will play Hamilton and duel seven Jeffersons.

    Press the arrow keys to move.

    Press spacebar to shoot.

    Fail to dodge the Jeffersons, and you die.

    Shoot all the Jeffersons, and you win!
